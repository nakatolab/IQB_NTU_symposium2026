## 過去のセミナー

### 第20回 （2025年9月2日（火） 14:00-15:00）
#### Speaker: Dr. Wan Kin AU YEUNG (International Christian University)
タイトル: Epigenome and Chromatin Architecture Rewiring in Mammalian Oocyte Development

要旨: <br>
The mammalian oocyte undergoes epigenomic and three-dimensional (3D) chromatin reorganization that establishes cell identity and primes embryogenesis. This seminar provides an overview on key molecular milestones of oocyte growth (DNA methylation dynamics, histone modification landscapes, and 3D genome architecture), and highlights recent advances. Referencing to prior contribution, this seminar also introduces our ongoing work that integrates bioinformatics and machine-learning models to map regulatory network and infer causality.
